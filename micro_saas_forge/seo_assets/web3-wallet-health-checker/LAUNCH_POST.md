Hey everyone, I just built a tool to help check the health of an Ethereum wallet without needing to connect it.

It’s called the Web3 Wallet Health Checker. The idea is to let you quickly assess a wallet’s security posture, token distribution, and gas efficiency—all while keeping your privacy intact since you never have to connect or sign anything. Just paste in a public address.

I often found myself wanting a snapshot like this before interacting with a wallet, so I built it for my own use and thought others might find it handy.

You can try it out here:  
https://web3-wallet-health-checker-ki4z9s63e-tiankongmumus-projects.vercel.app

I’d really appreciate any feedback, especially if you spot bugs or have ideas for other metrics to include. Thanks for checking it out.
