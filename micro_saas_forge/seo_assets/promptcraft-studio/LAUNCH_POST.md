I just launched **Promptcraft Studio**, a side project I built to solve a problem I kept running into: crafting effective prompts for AI tools like ChatGPT and Midjourney is harder than it looks. I’d often waste time tweaking prompts and still get suboptimal results.

This is a simple, free tool to help structure and refine your prompts with a focus on clarity and intent. It’s not magic, but it provides a framework that has worked well for me.

I’d love for you to try it and share any feedback. It’s early days, and I’m hoping to improve it based on what the community thinks.
