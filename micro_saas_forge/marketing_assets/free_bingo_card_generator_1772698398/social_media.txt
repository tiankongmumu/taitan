=== REDDIT POST ===
**I was so frustrated with how clunky and ad-ridden free bingo card generators were, so I built a cleaner one for myself.**

I needed to make custom bingo cards for a family game night, and every free tool I tried either had terrible UX, forced watermarks, or buried the print/PDF option behind a paywall. I just wanted to drag items around, see a live preview, and export something clean—without jumping through hoops.

So I built a **free bingo card generator** with:
- A modern, drag‑and‑drop UI to arrange items freely
- Real‑time card preview as you edit
- Direct printing and PDF export with no watermarks, even on the free tier

Tech-wise, it’s a Next.js 14 app with TypeScript, Tailwind for styling, and react‑dnd for the drag interactions. The PDF generation is handled client‑side using jsPDF so nothing gets sent to a server.

If you ever need to whip up bingo cards for events, classrooms, or just for fun, you can try it here:  
[https://shipmicro.com/tools](https://shipmicro.com/tools)

I’d love some brutal feedback—especially on the UX or if you run into any quirks with the PDF output. Thanks in advance.
