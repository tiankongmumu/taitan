=== REDDIT POST ===
**I built a crypto airdrop finder because I was tired of manually checking eligibility for every new drop.**

As a dev who dabbles in crypto, I kept missing out on airdrops or wasting time on ones I wasn’t eligible for. I’d see a new project, dig through their docs, check my wallet history, and still not be sure. It felt like doing unpaid data entry.

So I made a tool that automatically scans your wallet against upcoming airdrop criteria and shows you what you qualify for. It also helps automate the claim process for supported drops—no more racing against transaction queues.

**How it works under the hood:**
- Connects via WalletConnect (we never see your keys)
- Fetches and parses on-chain activity (EVM chains for now)
- Cross-references with a curated list of drop criteria (sourced from public docs and community updates)
- For eligible claims, it can generate and submit the claim transaction on your behalf (you still sign it)

Built with Next.js 14, TypeScript, Tailwind, and a sprinkle of Viem for chain interactions. Hosted on Vercel.

If you’re into airdrops or just hate manual eligibility checks, give it a try:  
[https://shipmicro.com/tools](https://shipmicro.com/tools)

I’d love some brutal feedback—especially on the UX, false positives/negatives in eligibility, or ideas for supporting more chains.
