Understanding the Problem: Why It’s Hard to Identify Your Visitors

The first step in solving any problem is understanding why it exists. In the case of SaaS businesses, the main issue is the lack of visibility into who is visiting your site. Without this information, it's nearly impossible to create personalized experiences, which are crucial for conversion.

Imagine if you could see exactly who is on your website, what pages they are visiting, and what actions they are taking. This level of insight would allow you to tailor your content, offers, and user experience to meet their specific needs. Unfortunately, most analytics tools only provide surface-level data, leaving you in the dark about the real identities and intentions of your visitors.
